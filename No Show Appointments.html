Introduction¶

This data set is a collection of data from 100k medical appintments in Brazil and is focused on the question whether or not the patients show up for their appointment.The dataset gives a description of patient's age,gender,medical history,Alcoholism.It also gives the complete data about appointment details the date,whether the sms was send and finally it tells that the patients showed up for the appointment or not.

Questions to be asked??¶

1.Does age plays a role in whether the patient shows up?
2.What percentage of people didn't show up for appoitment?
3.Which gender does show up generally?
4.On which days of the week do most of the patients show up?
5.Which neighborhoods have the highest number of shows/no-shows?
6.Are sms reminders helping more patients to turn up for the appointment?
7.Does enrolling a scholarship results in more patients showing up for appointment?

def print_1d_analysis(column_name, value, specific_text):
    total = len(df.index)
    total_value = (df[column_name] == value).sum()
    percent = int((total_value / total) * 100)
    text = ' This is ' + str(percent) + '% of ' + str(total) + ' records.'
    print(str(total_value) + ' ' + specific_text + text)

def show_per(column_names, values,texts):

  • Age groups 0-20,20-30 showed least in amounts for the appointments.
  • We found that nearly 80% of the patients showed up for the appointments,which is good.
  • Gender was not quite a parameter to compare because the percentage of females showing up for the appoitments was almost same with the percentage of male showing up.
  • The majority of the patients not showing up where from 'JARDIM CAMBURI' ,we need to find the reason why patients didn't show quiet in number from this neighbourhood.
  • Most of the no-shows for the appointments were on Tuesdays.
  • Sending SMS helped in patients showing up for the appointments ,around 72.4% of the patients showed.So more and more SMS should be sent to patients.
  • Enrolling in scholarship didn't help in more patients showing up as compared to patients who haven't enrolled for the scholarship have showed up in great percentage.
